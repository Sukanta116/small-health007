Built from the
Ground Up

Small Heath is not a brand. It's a territory. A mindset. A refusal to accept anything that isn't built right.

The Story

Started with one design and one conviction — that the streets deserve better than what they're getting. No investor decks. No mood boards. Just fabric, a press, and a standard that doesn't move.

Every piece asks one question: would someone wear this every day without thinking twice?

The Standard

  • No fast fashion. Every piece survives years, not seasons.
  • 380gsm minimum. We don't do thin.
  • Fair production. We know every hand that touches the cloth.
  • No trends. What we make looks right five years from now.
  • Real sizing. Tested on real bodies. Not mannequins.

The Fabric

100% ring-spun cotton from certified mills. 380gsm standard — nearly double the industry average. It drapes different. Feels different. You'll know the first time you put it on.

The Rule

We drop twice a year. That's it. No constant refreshes. No chasing algorithms. When it's gone, it's gone. We'd rather run out than put our name on something that isn't built right.
